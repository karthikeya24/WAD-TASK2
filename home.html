Instagram is a popular social media platform that allows users to share photos and videos with their followers. It was launched in October 2010 and quickly gained widespread popularity, especially among younger users.

Instagram enables users to upload photos and videos, apply filters to them, and share them on their profile or with a select group of people.

Users can follow other accounts to see their posts in their feed, and they can also interact with posts by liking, commenting, and sharing them. Instagram has features like Stories, IGTV (Instagram TV), and Reels, which offer additional ways for users to share content.
