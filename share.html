Instagram is a free app that allows users to share photos and videos with their followers or a select group of friends.



To post a photo or video on Instagram, you can:

  1. Tap the + icon at the top of the screen

  2. Choose a photo or video from your library or shoot one in the app

  3. Crop the image

  4. Pick a filter

  5. Edit your photo

  6. Type your caption

  7. Use hashtags for post optimization
